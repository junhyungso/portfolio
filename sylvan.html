Sylvan Nails Business Website

Junhyung So
Created July 21, 2019

I created a website for a local nail salon business using Squarespace, incorporating my knowledge in Photoshop and CSS. The owner of the business contacted me, wishing to improve its popularity and show off its unique nail treatments. We decided that the creation of a clean, appealing website and allowing easier access to our website from Google searches was a good place to start. By gathering pictures of the business interiors, its customers, and testimonials, I was able to produce such website.

Please check out the site at: SYLVAN NAILS and let me know if you have any questions or suggestions to improve the website!
